Welcome to

Strawberry

Strawberry is the first comprehensive online grocery store in Buraidah. You will find everything you are looking for. Right from fresh fruits and vegetables, poultry, meat and dairy products online. we have it all. Save time and money, shop at Strawberry.

It is very simple and easy. No more standing in lines, carrying heavy bags and looking for parking! Get started with online grocery shopping at Buraidah’s favorite online grocery store. Choose from a wide range of options in every groceries list, exclusively handpicked to help you find the best quality available at the lowest prices. Select a time slot for home delivery and your order will be delivered to your doorstep, anywhere in Buraidah. You can pay by cash, card or Sadad. We guarantee on-time delivery and the best quality! Happy Shopping!

We, at Strawberry, are excited about making life simpler and grocery shopping easy! Now you can buy fresh fruits and vegetables, poultry, meat and dairy products at your leisure and from the comfort of your home - no more standing in the long queues at ration shops, provision stores & supermarkets. We made it easy & simple when you are shopping groceries at Strawberry.
